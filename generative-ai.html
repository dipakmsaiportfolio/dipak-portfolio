My Generative AI Journey

My interest in Generative AI grew significantly when ChatGPT was launched. The capabilities of conversational AI fascinated me, and I began experimenting with ChatGPT on my own—exploring its potential for problem-solving, creativity, and automation. These hands-on experiences sparked a deeper curiosity about how AI models work and how they can be applied across industries. As my passion for AI evolved, I decided to take a formal step toward mastering this field. In Fall 2025, I enrolled in the Master’s program in Applied Artificial Intelligence at Utah Valley University. This program will allow me to gain advanced knowledge in machine learning, deep learning, and generative AI technologies, preparing me to contribute meaningfully to the future of AI-driven innovation..
